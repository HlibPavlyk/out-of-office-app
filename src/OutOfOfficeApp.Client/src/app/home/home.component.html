Welcome to the Out Of Office system!

This system is designed to help manage employee out of office schedules and project assignments. With this system, you can:

  • Add new employees to the system
  • Manage existing employee schedules
  • View and manage approval requests for out of office schedules
  • Assign employees to projects or unassign them as needed
  • View a calendar of all out of office schedules
  • And much more!

Explore the features and see how it can simplify your scheduling and project management tasks.
